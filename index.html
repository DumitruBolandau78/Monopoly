How to Play

    Starting the Game

  • Each player starts with a set amount of money ($1500) and receives two dice.

    • Turns

  • On your turn, roll both dice and move your token clockwise around the board the number of spaces indicated by the dice total.
  • Depending on the space you land on, you may take different actions
  • Property: If the space is unowned, you have the option to buy it. If you choose not to buy it or cannot afford it, the property goes up for auction.
  • Valar Morghulis or The Iron Throne: Draw a card and follow the instructions.
  • Jail: If you land on the "Go to Jail" space, draw a card or roll doubles on your next turn to get out of jail.
  • Income Tax or Luxury Tax: Pay the specified amount to the bank.
  • Free Parking: No action is taken.

    • Property Ownership

  • If you choose to buy a property, pay the listed purchase price to the bank and receive the property's deed.
  • If you land on a property owned by another player, pay them the specified rent amount.
  • If you own all the properties of a color group, you can collect higher rent from opponents who land on those properties.

    • Building Houses and Hotels

  • Once you own all properties in a color group, you can purchase houses and hotels to increase the rent.
  • The cost of houses and hotels is listed on the property deeds.
  • You must build houses evenly across all properties in a color group before purchasing hotels.

    • Trading and Auctions

  • Players can trade properties, money, and other assets with each other.
  • If a player declines to buy an unowned property they land on, it goes up for auction, and players can bid to purchase it.

    • Bankruptcy and Game End

  • If a player cannot afford to pay rent, taxes, or other debts, they are declared bankrupt.
  • The game continues until only one player remains solvent. That player is the winner.
